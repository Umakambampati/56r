PORTFOLIO

A portfolio is a collection of work that showcases an individual's skills, achievements, and experiences in a particular field. It is commonly used by students, professionals, artists, designers, and developers to present their best work to potential employers or clients. A well-organized portfolio reflects a person's strengths, creativity, and growth over time. It often includes projects, certificates, resumes, and sometimes personal reflections. Digital portfolios are widely used today as they are easy to access and share online. A strong portfolio helps in building credibility and confidence during job interviews or academic applications. It allows others to understand your style, process, and problem-solving ability. Portfolios can be tailored for different goals, such as job applications, college admissions, or freelance opportunities. Regularly updating your portfolio ensures that it stays relevant and current.
